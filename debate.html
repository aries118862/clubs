Welcome to the Debate Club

Persuasion, critical thinking, and eloquent speaking await you in the Student Debate Club! Whether you're a confident speaker or just learning to express your ideas, this is your platform to shine. Participate in debates, public speaking sessions, and workshops while collaborating with peers who share your passion for discussion and argumentation. No experience? No problem. Bring your curiosity and confidence—we’ll help you sharpen your voice. Step up to the podium: your debating journey starts here.
